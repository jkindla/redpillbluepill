About

Grey-scale songs with flashes of red and blue.

Red Pill Blue Pill makes guitar-forward music with a stripped-back visual sensibility: direct, moody, and focused on the performance. The band leans into contrast, pairing intimate verses with wide-open choruses and a live show that keeps the room close.
